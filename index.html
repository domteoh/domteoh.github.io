Out of the Shadows

Out of the Shadows is a short documentary about Quinto Imperio, a Cumbia band from Chicago’s Back of the Yards neighbourhood. It is a story of immigrants who are undocumented, unapologetic, and unafraid to fight for their future and for the future of youth from a community that is often misunderstood.

Co-creators: Amanda Hermans, Caroline Olsen

About
Hide

Dominique is a filmmaker, primarily interested in factual content. He is a senior at Northwestern University, where he studies Radio/TV/Film and Political Science. Born and raised in Malaysia to Chinese and Swiss parents, and having studied in Italy and the United States, he is often drawn to issues of migration and cultural identity. He knows some things, but there's also a lot he doesn't know, so he continues to learn from those around him.

If the camera's not rolling, his skateboard is.
